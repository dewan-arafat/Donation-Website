Our Donate Plan

Our activities are divided into some categories. Here some of our donate plan. Besides, we have some other voluntary activities.

Food for a family

In a world of plenty, hunger should be a thing of past. Across the country, many people do not have enough food.Our foundation works on various activities in seeking solutions to hunger.

Shelter for a family

Many people of our country are homeless. They spend their life under the open sky. A part of our activities is to ensure shelter of those homeless people.

Education for a child

Education is the backbone of a nation. To ensure this basic need, we spend a portion of your donated money for those people who are unable to bear the expenditure.

Work as a Volunteer

Our foundation operates various types of voluntary servieces like working for flood affected people, building house for homeless people, tree plantation etc. We are eagarly waiting for some active volunteer members.
